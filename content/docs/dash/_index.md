Documentation for customer (tenant) admins. This section covers managing your organisation's users, mailboxes, groups, and Nextcloud access through the lintune-dash portal.

Your portal is scoped to your own organisation — you cannot access other tenants' data.

If you're an MSP operator setting up the platform, see the [Admin](../admin) section.
